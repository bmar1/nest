Design Foundation
Color Palette Rationale:

Forest Green (#2D5016): Primary color evoking "nest" and home stability - darker, premium feel
Sage (#87A96B): Calming complement for secondary actions
Cream (#FAF6F1): Warm background that's easier on eyes than pure white
Charcoal/Slate: Softer text hierarchy than harsh black

Typography:

Inter: Modern, screen-optimized sans-serif for both headings and body (keeps it cohesive)
Fira Code: Monospace for prices/numbers so they align vertically for easy scanning

Design Philosophy:

Clarity Over Complexity - Remove cognitive load during a stressful apartment search
Trust Through Transparency - Show matching logic openly since users are making life decisions
Speed Without Sacrifice - Use the 1-3 min wait time productively with engaging progress states

What's Inside

Complete component library (buttons, inputs, cards, badges)
Apartment listing card specs with match score visualization
Progress indicator for scraping states
Responsive breakpoints and grid system
Accessibility guidelines (WCAG 2.1 AA compliant)
CSS custom properties/design tokens
Animation standards
